Katisha Online

Revolutionizing Inter-District Travel in Rwanda

About Katisha Online

Elevator Pitch

Katisha Online is a mobile app that allows users to easily find, compare, and book inter-district bus tickets across Rwanda in just a few taps. Our seamless booking and e-ticketing system eliminates long queues and hassles, while our smart routing helps travelers reach their destination quickly and affordably.

Detailed Description

Katisha Online provides a seamless and user-friendly online booking experience that puts convenience and efficiency at the forefront. Users can easily search for available routes, compare transport options, and secure their tickets with just a few clicks, eliminating the need to physically stand in long queues.

Convenience at Your Fingertips

Imagine never having to wait in long, frustrating queues just to buy a bus ticket for inter-district travel in Rwanda. With Katisha Online, you can book your inter-district bus tickets from anywhere, anytime, with just a few taps on your mobile device or computer.

Addressable Market

Based on the available data, Rwanda has a population of approximately 13.25 million people. Assuming that 50% of the population uses public transportation for inter-district travel, we can estimate the total number of users to be around 6.63 million people.

Differentiating Factors

Katisha Online is the first platform of its kind in Rwanda, providing a convenient and eco-friendly solution for commuters seeking efficient and accessible ticketing options for their inter-district travels.

Key Features

Search and Filtering

Search for available tickets and routes and filter them based on your preferences.

Online Booking

Book your tickets online with ease and convenience.

E-Ticketing

Receive your tickets instantly and avoid queues.

Our Inspiration

It all started one day when we saw the long queues at bus stations and thought, "There has to be a better way." Our team, consisting of frequent travelers, decided to take on this challenge. We wanted to make travel in Rwanda more convenient and efficient. Thus, Katisha Online was born. This project is not just a requirement for Holberton School, but a solution to a real-world problem we are passionate about solving.

Our journey began with brainstorming sessions, market research, and endless cups of coffee. The idea evolved over time, and with the support of Holberton School, we turned our vision into reality. You can view our progress and contributions on our GitHub repository.
